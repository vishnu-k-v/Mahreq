CANADA

Welcome to Canada’s Permanent Residency Program!

MAHREQ warmly welcomes individuals from around the globe. Our Permanent Residency Program for Canada is thoughtfully designed to provide skilled workers, investors, entrepreneurs, and their families with the opportunity to make Canada their permanent home

Starting the journey towards Permanent Residency may seem complex, but rest assured, our experienced Migration consultants are here to guide you. We’ll help you navigate the entire process, ensuring that all requirements are met and your application is complete and accurate. Your Canadian permanent residency is within reach – let us lead you to the next chapter of your life.

  • BENEFITS
    Becoming a permanent resident of Canada opens the door to numerous benefits, such as:
    • Healthcare:Permanent resident, you and your family will have access to free essential healthcare services, including doctor visits, hospital stays, and surgeries, funded by taxes.
    • Education:Permanent resident of Canada, you’ll benefit from Canada’s top-tier education system with significantly reduced tuition fees compared to international students
    • Employment: As a permanent resident of Canada, you have the freedom to live and work anywhere in the country and the opportunity to apply for Canadian citizenship.
    • Social Benefits: Permanent residents of Canada are eligible for various social benefits, such as retirement and disability benefits, unemployment insurance, and child benefits.
    • Diversity and Inclusivity: Canada is renowned for its inclusive, diverse society. As a permanent resident, you’ll have the chance to join and contribute to this vibrant multicultural community

  • PROGRAMS
    Canada's Permanent Residency Program offers various categories, each with unique eligibility criteria and specific requirements.
    • Express Entry: This category is designed for skilled workers seeking permanent residency in Canada. To qualify, applicants must meet the criteria for one of three federal economic immigration programs: the Federal Skilled Worker Program, the Federal Skilled Trades Program, or the Canadian Experience Class.
    • Provincial Nominee Program (PNP): This category enables Canadian provinces and territories to nominate individuals who align with their unique economic and labor market requirements. Each province and territory operates its own Provincial Nominee Program (PNP).
    • Family Sponsorship: This category permits Canadian citizens and permanent residents to sponsor eligible family members for permanent residency in Canada.
    • Business Immigration: This category is designed for individuals interested in starting or investing in a business in Canada. It includes various programs such as the Start-up Visa Program, the Self-employed Persons Program, and the Investor Program.

AUSTRALIA

Welcome to Australia’s Permanent Residency Program!

The Australia Permanent Residency Program can make your dream a reality. As a permanent resident, you’ll immerse yourself in Australia’s stunning landscapes, benefit from exceptional education and healthcare systems, and enjoy a high quality of life in a vibrant, multicultural community. Our efficient pathways open doors to Australia’s thriving economy, offering endless opportunities for professional success and personal development.

The permanent residency application process can be complex and overwhelming, but our team of skilled migration agents is here to guide you every step of the way. From assessing your eligibility to managing visa applications, we are committed to helping you achieve your dream of becoming a permanent resident of Australia.

  • BENEFITS

    Benefits of Becoming a Permanent Resident of Australia

    • Work and Study: As a permanent resident, you are free to work and study anywhere in Australia without limitations and have access to government-funded education and training programs.
    • Health Care: You can access Australia's public healthcare system, Medicare, which offers medical services and subsidized prescription medications.
    • Social Security: Eligibility for social security benefits, including unemployment, sickness, and parenting payments, may be available.
    • Citizenship: After residing in Australia for a specified period as a permanent resident, you may become eligible to apply for Australian citizenship.
    • Travel: As a permanent resident, you have the freedom to travel in and out of Australia as often as you wish and are eligible to apply for an Australian passport.
    • Family Sponsorship: Once you become a permanent resident, you can sponsor your family members to come and live in Australia.
    • Quality of Life: Experience Australia's high standard of living, welcoming and safe communities, and stunning natural landscapes, all of which contribute to an enhanced quality of life for you and your family.

  • PROGRAMS
    Australia Permanent Residency Program Options
    • Skilled Independent Visa (Subclass 189): The Subclass 189 visa lets skilled workers live and work permanently in Australia without sponsorship or nomination, for those with skills on the skilled occupation list and who meet the points criteria. It offers flexibility and a pathway to permanent residency.
    • Skilled Nominated Visa (Subclass 190): This program is similar to the Skilled Independent Visa but requires a nomination from a state or territory government. If you have a specific state or territory in mind, this could be the ideal option for you.
    • Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme (Subclass 187): This program aims to attract skilled workers to live and work in regional areas of Australia. To qualify, you must have an employer willing to sponsor you and meet the necessary skills and qualifications requirements.
    • Business Talent (Permanent) Visa (Subclass 132): This program is for business owners, investors, and entrepreneurs who wish to start or grow a business in Australia. To be eligible, you must have a proven track record in business and demonstrate the ability to make a significant contribution to the Australian economy.
    • Partner Visa (Subclass 820/801): This program is for individuals in a genuine, ongoing relationship with an Australian citizen or permanent resident. To qualify, you must meet specific criteria and provide evidence of your relationship.

NEW ZEALAND

Welcome to New Zealand Permanent Residency Program!

The New Zealand Permanent Residency Program offers a pathway to make your dreams come true. As a permanent resident, you’ll experience the country's stunning natural beauty, benefit from top-tier education and healthcare, and enjoy a high standard of living in a dynamic and multicultural society. Our streamlined programs provide access to New Zealand's robust economy, unlocking numerous opportunities for career growth and personal fulfillment.

Navigating the complexities of the permanent residency application process can be challenging, but our team of experienced migration specialists is here to support you every step of the way. From assessing your eligibility to guiding you through the visa process, we are dedicated to helping you achieve your goal of becoming a permanent resident of New Zealand.

  • BENEFITS
    Benefits of New Zealand Permanent Residency
    • Work and Study Opportunities: Benefit from the ability to work and study in New Zealand without any limitations, allowing you to fully explore career opportunities and educational pursuits.
    • Public Healthcare Access: Qualify for access to New Zealand's public healthcare system, which provides essential medical services and subsidized prescription medications.
    • Social Security Benefits: Access to social security benefits, including unemployment assistance and various welfare programs.
    • Citizenship Pathway: The opportunity to apply for New Zealand citizenship once you fulfill the residency requirements.
    • Travel Flexibility: The freedom to travel in and out of New Zealand without restrictions, offering greater flexibility and ease of movement.
    • Family Reunification: The option to sponsor eligible family members for permanent residency, facilitating family reunification.
    • Quality of Life: Experience New Zealand's high standard of living, secure communities, and breathtaking natural landscapes.
    • Diverse and Inclusive Society: Become part of a diverse and inclusive society that values and celebrates cultural differences.
    • Education Access: Access New Zealand's world-class education system, often at reduced rates for permanent residents.
    • Business and Investment Opportunities: Opportunities for business ownership, entrepreneurship, and investment in New Zealand's thriving economy.

  • PROGRAMS
    New Zealand Permanent Residency Programs
    • Skilled Migrant Category (SMC): Designed for skilled workers with in-demand qualifications and experience in New Zealand, this program uses a points-based system that takes into account factors such as age, work experience, and qualifications.
    • Residence from Work (RFW): This program is intended for temporary work visa holders who have been employed in New Zealand and wish to transition to permanent residency.
    • Investor Program: This program allows individuals with substantial funds to invest in New Zealand to apply for permanent residency, with varying investment thresholds and criteria depending on the specific program.
    • Entrepreneur Work Visa: This program is for individuals with a proven business track record who wish to establish or purchase a business in New Zealand, potentially leading to permanent residency.
    • Family Categories: This program allows family members of New Zealand citizens or permanent residents to apply for permanent residency, with categories including partners, dependent children, and parents.
    • Parent Retirement Category: This program offers a pathway for parents to reunite with their adult children in New Zealand, provided they meet certain financial and health requirements.
    • Refugee Family Support Category: This program allows refugees settled in New Zealand to sponsor their family members to join them, facilitating family reunification.
    • Pacific Access Category: This program offers a limited number of places each year for citizens of specific Pacific Island nations to apply for permanent residency in New Zealand.
    • Samoa Quota Scheme: This program is specifically for citizens of Samoa, allowing them to apply for permanent residency through a quota system.
    • Talent (Accredited Employers) Work Visa: This program targets highly skilled workers who have a job offer from an accredited New Zealand employer and can lead to permanent residency.

  • BENEFITS
    Schengen Permanent Residency Benefits
    • Freedom of Movement: Enjoy unrestricted travel within the Schengen Area, allowing seamless movement across borders without the need for internal border controls.
    • Residence Flexibility: The ability to reside in any of the Schengen countries, offering flexibility to choose your place of residence based on personal preferences.
    • Work Opportunities: Access to employment opportunities across all Schengen countries without the need for additional work permits.
    • Business and Investment: Facilitation of business and investment activities across Schengen countries, encouraging greater economic engagement and opportunities.
    • Education Access: Eligibility to enroll in educational institutions within the Schengen Area, often at reduced rates or favorable conditions for permanent residents.
    • Healthcare Benefits: Access to healthcare services in any Schengen country, ensuring comprehensive medical care whenever needed.
    • Social Security Coverage: Eligibility for social security benefits, including retirement and disability benefits, unemployment assistance, and family-related support.
    • Family Reunification: The possibility to sponsor eligible family members for permanent residency, facilitating family reunification within the Schengen Area.
    • Cultural Integration: Integration into a diverse cultural environment, promoting cultural exchange and fostering understanding within the Schengen community.
    • Pathway to Citizenship: In some Schengen countries, permanent residency can serve as a pathway to citizenship, providing a long-term connection to the local community.

  • PROGRAMS
    Schengen Permanent Residency Programs
    • Long-Term Residency Permit: Many Schengen countries offer long-term residency permits to individuals who have lived in the country for an extended period.
    • Entrepreneur/Investor Visa: Residency programs for entrepreneurs and investors who contribute to the country's economy through business ventures and investment activities.
    • Skilled Worker Programs:Programs for skilled workers with qualifications and expertise in high-demand fields.
    • Family Reunification: Residency granted to family members of current residents or citizens, facilitating family reunification.
    • Student Visa to PR: The opportunity to transition from a student visa to long-term residency for individuals who have successfully completed their studies.
